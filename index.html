Who This Is For

Designed for the whole creative.

Feybl is built around the reality that creativity and wellbeing aren't separate. Our practitioners are carefully selected to serve the unique needs of artists, freelancers, and anyone doing meaningful work.

  • Artists and creatives seeking physical recovery alongside their practice
  • Freelancers experiencing burnout or body tension from long hours
  • Members wanting to deepen their inner work alongside studio time
  • Anyone curious about holistic modalities in a non-clinical, welcoming space
  • Professionals who want integrative care that respects their schedule

Questions

Common asks.

Do I need to be a Feybl member to book?

No — sessions are open to the public. Members receive priority booking and may have credits included in their plan.

How do I book with a specific practitioner?

Each practitioner card links directly to their individual booking page. You'll see their real-time availability and can book in minutes.

Where do sessions take place?

All sessions are held in our private studio rooms at Feybl House in Burbank. Exact address provided at booking confirmation.

Can I combine a session with the wellness amenities?

Yes — members can access the sauna, cold plunge, red light therapy, and more on the same visit. Ask at the front desk about add-ons.

I'm a practitioner — how do I join?

Reach out to the Feybl team directly. We're selective but actively growing, and love working with practitioners who understand the creative community.
